QUICK RULES
The People's Choice Qualifier is open to any team from North or South America.
The top eight eligible teams at the close of the People's Choice Qualifier (29 Oct 2017 23:59:59 US ET) will be selected for the main event.
The main event is a double-elimination bracket seeded by vote count.
All Dota 2 games are captains mode. All Bo3 series are best of three (Bo3), except the final which is a best of five (Bo5). All players must be 18 years old or older.
