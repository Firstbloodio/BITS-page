Step 1: Assemble your squad!

Round up the 5-7 best Dota 2 players you know and convince them to join your team.

Step 2: Declare your team's eligibility for B.I.T.S.

Use your team's twitter account to declare your entry into the tournament by tweeting something like this.

"This is our entry ticket to @firstbloodio's #BITS2017! retweet to vote us in, top 32 most retweeted teams get to play. #Dota2"

(make sure to include @firstbloodio and #BITS2017)

Step 3: Rally your fans

Tweet it, knock on doors, blast your contacts, shout it from roof tops, run giveaways. Whatever you gotta do to do to get your fans to hit that retweet button.

The top 32 most retweeted teams get to play, so your ability to rally the support of your fans is going to be crucial.

Step 4: Bring your A-game

It's called Blood in the Streets for a reason.

B.I.T.S. is an unforgiving single-elimination tournament.

To win it all your team will have to be unrelenting, unforgiving, and willing to leave a path of your concured opponents in your wake.

How it works

B.I.T.S. is open to everyone who can assemble a team and rally their fans to hit the retweet button.
The top 32 most retweeted teams will be seeded into B.I.T.S in order of the number of retweets they get.

  • Winner takes all

  • Loads of Other Prizes
