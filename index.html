Step 1: Assemble your squad!

The first 8 teams will be invited by us.
The next 8 spots are up for grabs by teams from the community.
Round up the 5-7 best Dota 2 ™ players you know and convince them to join your team.

Step 2: Declare your team's eligibility

Use your team's twitter account to declare your entry into the tournament by tweeting something like this.

"This is our entry ticket to @firstbloodio's #Dota2BITS!"

(make sure to include @firstbloodio and #Dota2BITS)

Step 3: Rally your fans

Tweet it, knock on doors, blast your contacts, shout it from roof tops, run giveaways. Whatever you gotta do to do to get your fans to make us notice you.

We'll select the 8 teams with the most fan support, so your ability to whip your fans into a frenzy going to be crucial.

Step 4: Bring your A-game

It's called Blood in the Streets™ for a reason.

B.I.T.S. ™ is an unforgiving single-elimination tournament.

To win it all your team will have to be unrelenting, unforgiving, and willing to leave a bloody path of your concured opponents in your wake.

How it works

B.I.T.S. ™ is open to everyone who can assemble a team and rally their fans.
Our 8 invited teams will play off against the 8 community nomminated teams in a single elimination tournament.

  • Winner takes all

    With a $5,000 prize pool up for grabs, the team that manages to emerge victorious will take home some serious cash.

  • Loads of Other Prizes

    On top of the regular prize pool, each round will have a selection (MVP, play of the round, etc) of other prizes awarded by popular vote.

  • Live on twitch

    Every game will be broadcast live on twitch.
    The first three rounds hosted by community casters,
    with the final round hosted by a big name studio/casting team.

  • Communtiy Casters Bracket

    8 Teams of community casters will also be competing for your love and admiration, along with their own prize pool.
